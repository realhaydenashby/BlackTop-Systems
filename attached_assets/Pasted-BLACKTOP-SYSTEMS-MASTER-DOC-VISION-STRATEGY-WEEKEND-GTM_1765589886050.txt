Step 2: The Math Engine (Algorithm-First)
Here's where we're different. Before any AI touches the data, our system runs deterministic calculations - burn rate, runway, cash flow projections, growth rates. These are exact numbers, not guesses. We use statistical methods (moving averages, anomaly detection, Monte Carlo simulations) to identify patterns and flag unusual spending.
Why does this matter? Because when we tell a founder "your runway dropped by 2.3 months," that's a fact from math, not a hallucination from an AI model.
Step 3: AI Interprets the Math
Only after the math is done does our AI layer kick in. We run a multi-model ensemble - OpenAI, Groq, and Gemini working together. If one model is down or gives a weird answer, the others catch it. Think of it like having three smart analysts cross-check each other.
The AI's job isn't to calculate - it's to explain what the numbers mean and suggest what to do about it specific to their business
Step 4: Initiative, Not Queries
This is the killer feature. Most financial tools are passive dashboards - they wait for you to ask questions. BlackTop interrupts you with truth.
* "Your AWS costs spiked 47% this month. Here's why."
* "At current burn, you have 8.2 months of runway. Here's an action plan."

We call these initiatives - proactive insights that pop up the moment something changes, with a one-click action plan.
